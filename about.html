About Us

Background

XL Sweep is a platform that was created by a team of developers and designers in fulfillment of the Zuri training requirement. The platform was created to provide a simple user friendly solution for comparing excel/spreadsheet files easily. It is targeted at users who regularly work with excel files and need a simple way to compare files or documents with similar contents, check for differences and make simple changes like remove duplicates, remove empty cells, merge two files into one and download or save the file for future use.
